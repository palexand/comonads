Comonad.LVS
Description
LVS comonad based on the presentation of T. Uustalu and V. Vene |The Essence of Dataflow Programming. Note that List in the |original presentation is replaced by Past to disambiguate from |standard Haskell List. The constructor :| has been replaced by :! |due to issues with lhs2TeX.

data Past a
Past is essentially a list where cons adds to the end rather than |the beginning.

data LV a
LV is a value composed with a Past representing stream |transformations that have been performed. It's okay that Past is |finite because it represents what we have seen so far.

data LVS a
LVS is an LV comonad with a Stream representing future stream |transformations in addition to past stream transformations. The |future must be a Stream because we don't have an oracle to tell us |how long the system will run.
